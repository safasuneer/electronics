About Us

Electronics offers a wide range of services as outlined in the detailed sections below. It is ultimately the goal of the company to offer a one-stop facility for all home entertainment needs, including both sales and servicing. In this way the company can offer greater perceived value for the customer than many other shops which only offer sales or services.

  • Authorized warranty servicing on all major brands of home entertainment systems.
  • Authorized warranty servicing on all major brands
  • House calls and free pickup and delivery.

Electronics will be obtaining most of its parts through established dealers and directly through the manufacturers of the relevant electronics. A large part of Electronics enhanced services will be free pickup and delivery of electronics to a persons home. The company will aggressively seek to procure its parts from local suppliers in order to start forming close relations with such companies.
